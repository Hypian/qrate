How We Work

Non-profit work deserves non-performative communication.

We don't parachute in, we don't over-promise, and we don't treat your beneficiaries as content.

We show up, we listen, and we deliver material your organisation is proud to put its name on.

01 —

Partner,
not provider.

We work with a small number of organisations deeply rather than many organisations thinly. Your brief has our principals on it — from kick-off to final cut.

02 —

Proof,
not posts.

We make work that survives beyond the feed — films, reports, and campaigns your funders and board members can hold up as evidence of what you do.

03 —

Dignity
first.

The people in your programmes are not characters in your story — they are the authors of theirs. Our work gives them the respect and the frame they deserve.
